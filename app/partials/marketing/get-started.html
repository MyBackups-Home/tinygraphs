GET STARTED

To use tinygraphs in your webside just set the source of your <img> element to the url of a tinygraphs image like so:

<img src="http://tinygraphs.com/squares/helloworld">

You can also choose to download your creation and upload it on your website. Just use our editor and then click on the download button.
